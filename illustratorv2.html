During the illustrator unit we learnt about different functions in Adobe Illustrator such as the pathfinder tool, extrude, plane, and revolve 3D effects, and also how to apply lighting & textures to 3D shapes.

These skills can be useful because Illustrator is an easy way to build custom 2D and 3D shapes, which could be used to make logos or icons.
